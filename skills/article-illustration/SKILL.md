# Article Illustration

Illustrations must map to article structure, not just topic keywords.

## Output

- section-level illustration plan
- purpose-aware still prompts
- image placement notes

## Rules

- identify where visuals help comprehension
- visualize the underlying concept, not just literal nouns
- write prompt files before generation
- prefer article-specific labels, metrics, and metaphors

## Workflow Integration

Packs should emit `modules/article-illustration-brief.md`.
This skill pairs naturally with `url-to-markdown`, `format-markdown`, and `markdown-to-html`.
