# Style Library

Epic fantasy banner generation for technical content. Each style is a prompt template — customize `[YOUR SUBJECT]` to match your post.

---

## ACIDBATH Aesthetic Reference

**Mood**: Epic, contemplative, mysterious. Grand scale with intimate human presence.

**Lighting**: Golden hour, lantern glow, chiaroscuro — warm amber light against cool dark shadows.

**Atmosphere**: Twilight, dawn, mist, fog, dust motes, smoke.

**Texture**: Aged stone, dark water, mist, weathered metal, ancient materials.

**Scale**: Massive elements (gears the size of buildings, vast underground chambers, mountain peaks) with small human figures for contrast.

**Colors**:
```
Deep teal:      #2a5a6a   (sky, water, shadows)
Dusk plum:      #8a5a6a   (accent, depth)
Golden amber:   #d4a574   (light, warmth)
Sandy ochre:    #c9a86c   (secondary warmth)
Warm cream:     #e8dcc4   (stone, parchment)
Dark indigo:    #1a1a2a   (deep background)
```

**Never**: neon, cyberpunk, flat tech diagrams, generic stock photo vibes.

## B1: The Gilded Mechanism

**Use when**: Code, engineering, systems, agents, tools, architecture.

**Vibe**: Massive floating gears and mechanical elements suspended in dark water. Ancient but precise. Epic serenity.

**Prompt**:
```
A single massive brass gear, thirty meters wide, suspended above dark teal water
at twilight. [YOUR SUBJECT] integrated subtly into the gear's surface — [DESCRIBE HOW].
In the distance, more gears fade into atmospheric haze. A tiny hooded figure
stands on the gear's edge, dwarfed by its scale. Golden amber light catches the
gear's teeth. Deep teal water below reflects the light. Dust motes float in
the golden beams. Cinematic, epic fantasy, hyper-detailed. Warm amber, deep
teal, dusk plum shadows. Atmospheric depth, visible brushwork. No text, no
logos, no futuristic elements. Mysterious, contemplative, grand.
```

**Example**: "circuit board patterns etched into the gear's surface in gold leaf"

**Service**: Codex CLI ($imagegen) or MiniMax CLI
**Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (2560x1440 output)

## PS: Painterly / Surrealist

**Use when**: Narrative depth, surreal logic, layered meaning.

**Reference**: The banners above are the reference. Albert Bierstadt landscape scale meets Zdzisław Beksiński surrealism meets Hayao Miyazaki detail.

**Prompt framework**:
```
[YOUR SCENE — describe the main subject and setting]

**Upper layer**: [Describe sky, light source, atmosphere]
**Middle layer**: [Describe main subject, scale, drama]
**Lower layer**: [Describe foreground, human presence, scale reference]

**Style**:
- Maximalist epic fantasy, hyper-detailed
- Chiaroscuro — golden internal glow vs dark shadow
- Atmospheric perspective — hazy distant elements
- Painterly oil paint aesthetic with visible brushwork
- Stippled texture, floating dust motes
- Deep teal #2a5a6a, dusk plum #8a5a6a, golden amber #d4a574, warm cream #e8dcc4

**Constraints**:
- NO cyberpunk, neon, tech HUD, circuit boards, holograms
- YES to steampunk, Victorian, mythic, surrealist, epic fantasy
- YES to painterly textures and atmospheric depth
```

**Service**: Codex CLI ($imagegen) or MiniMax CLI
**Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (2560x1440 output)
